PROJECT SCOPE

Understanding Culinary Demands & Platforms

This dashboard project analyzes real-world transaction patterns to answer fundamental questions about the operational efficiency of food ordering platforms. We synthesize millions of data points to evaluate driver assignments, platform ratings, and high-revenue food options.

Customer Behaviour

Decoding order frequency, peak purchase times, ratings, and meal options across multiple regions.

Cuisine Trends

Tracking high-volume categories like Asian, Continental, and American cuisines to isolate market preference shifts.

Delivery Performance

Measuring the operational throughput, average transit speeds, and weather impacts on arrival timelines.
